World Famous Cheese Toastie

The finest cheese toastie to grace this earth

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a pan.
  2. Place cheese between two slices of bread.
  3. Cook in melted butter.
  4. Add more butter if the bread soaks it all up.
  5. Fry until golden brown.
